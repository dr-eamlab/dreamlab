Our Research Projects

Ongoing and completed research initiatives addressing critical environmental challenges

2023-2024

Health Risk Assessment of Creek Water from Chittagong Hill Track, Bangladesh

This project assesses the health risks associated with creek water consumption in the Chittagong Hill Tracts region. We're evaluating water quality parameters, identifying contamination sources, and developing risk mitigation strategies for local communities.

Water Quality Analysis Health Risk Modeling GIS Mapping Community Surveys

Key Objectives:

  • Evaluate physicochemical and biological parameters of creek water
  • Assess heavy metal contamination and microbial pathogens
  • Develop health risk models for local populations
  • Create sustainable water management recommendations
2022-2023

Geochemical Study of Seawater Intrusion in Coastal Aquifers of the Bay of Bengal, Bangladesh

This research investigates seawater intrusion in coastal aquifers along the Bay of Bengal. We're analyzing hydrogeochemical processes, mapping intrusion patterns, and developing predictive models to safeguard freshwater resources.

Hydrogeochemistry Saltwater Intrusion Modeling Remote Sensing Geospatial Analysis

Key Objectives:

  • Monitor groundwater quality in coastal regions
  • Identify geochemical indicators of seawater intrusion
  • Develop predictive models for saltwater encroachment
  • Recommend sustainable groundwater management strategies
2021-2022

Spatio-temporal Variability of Noise Pollution in Urban Parks, Dhaka, Bangladesh

This project examines noise pollution patterns in Dhaka's urban parks across different times and seasons. We're mapping noise levels, identifying pollution sources, and assessing impacts on visitor experience and biodiversity.

Noise Monitoring Spatial Analysis Time-series Analysis Urban Ecology

Key Objectives:

  • Measure noise levels at multiple urban parks
  • Analyze temporal patterns (daily, weekly, seasonal)
  • Identify major noise pollution sources
  • Assess impact on visitor satisfaction and wildlife
  • Develop noise mitigation recommendations
2019-2020

Assessing Heavy Metal Concentration in Dhaka City Lake Water

This research evaluates heavy metal contamination in Dhaka's urban lakes. We're analyzing spatial distribution of pollutants, identifying contamination sources, and assessing ecological and public health risks.

Heavy Metal Analysis Water Pollution Assessment Risk Modeling Environmental Chemistry

Key Objectives:

  • Sample and analyze lake water from key urban locations
  • Measure concentrations of lead, cadmium, mercury, arsenic, etc.
  • Identify primary pollution sources and pathways
  • Assess ecological and human health risks
  • Develop remediation strategies for contaminated water bodies
